"Engine sound is far too dominating and got annoying after awhile. Other gameplay critical sounds didn't feel impactful."

"When using the pipeline shortcut that trigger cutscene, the camera might have bug that trigger a shaking and I have no idea where this pipeline will take me go."

"The camera would jump in and out of canned sequences pretty abruptly... I didn't feel I had control of the camera motion."
